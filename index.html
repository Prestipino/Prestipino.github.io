PlotTxrd

PlotTxrd is a graphical application for the visualization, analysis, and management of X-ray diffraction (XRD) data, with a particular focus on temperature-dependent experiments and high-throughput measurements typically collected at synchrotron beamlines.

The software handles a wide range of data formats (.xye, .dat, .raw, .uxd, .ras, .XrdML), and provides integrated tools for data inspection, background subtraction, merging, and export to JANA or FullProf formats.
